  • 关于CIFL

    成都国际足球联赛CIFL是8人制业余足球赛事，赛事诞生于2011年6月，至今已经历10个完整赛季（每年均分为春夏及秋冬两个赛季），每个赛季参赛球队数量在10-12支左右。
    赛事已超越足球本身，为外国人与中国人结识、锻炼以及通过赛事来展示快乐与竞争精神的绝佳平台；赛事目前拥有来自超过50个国家的200余名注册球员（堪称小世界杯）；球员年龄从18到45岁，也覆盖了很多不同职业，不同领域的人士，比如：教师、学生、本地的经理人以及外籍的CEO等等。

     

    The CIFL is an 8 a-side amateur soccer league based in Chengdu. It was established in June 2011 and has now played out 10 full seasons. It has between 10 and 12 teams competing each year split over two seasons. The league has become a place for expats and locals to meet, exercise and engage in sport in the spirit of fun and competition. The league currently has in excess of 200 registered players coming from over 50 countries from around the world. Players range in ages from 18-45, representing a huge cross-section of professions. There are teachers, students, local managers and foreign CEO's.
